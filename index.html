Badminton

Badminton is a court or lawn game played using lightweight racquets or rackets to hit a shuttlecock (also known as a “bird” or “birdie”) across a net that divides the court or lawn into two equal parts. There are several forms of the game but the most common ones are 'singles' (one player per side) and 'doubles' (two players per side). To enjoy the game's full potential, the game should be played indoors so that wind cannot affect the flight of the shuttlecock, however, the game can also be played in a yard or a beach as a casual outdoor activity.
